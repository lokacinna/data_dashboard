Why Trump's Online Advertising Strategy is Hard to Beat

The day after inauguration, President Trump relaunched his campaign freshly outfitted for 2020. While Democrats tend to focus on television ads and more traditional platforms for political advertising, the Trump campaign has created a tech-saavy style of bombardment ads to target the public's attention span for exactly as long as they're listening.


Google's Transparency Report offers a dashboard overview of the highest-paying advertisors, what states they spend the most money in, and how many ads they've paid for. We used this information to track President Trump's two current committees: the Make America Great Again Committee and Donald J Trump For President, Inc. Respectively, these are ranked as the first and sixth largest spenders in terms of political advertising on Google in the United States.

Between Donald J Trump for President, Inc. and the Make America Great Again Committee, $10,800,200 has been spent on 32,580 Google Ads since May 31st, 2018.
